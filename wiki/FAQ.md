# FAQ

## What is Sonic?

Sonic is the deployment and hardware bootstrap pathway for the repo family.

## Does Sonic own the `uHOME` contract?

No. `uHOME-server` is the canonical owner of `uHOME` bundle, preflight, and
install-plan contracts.

## What does Sonic own then?

Sonic owns planning, manifest generation, device-aware deployment, and the
Linux-side execution path that applies reviewed plans to hardware.

## Is this repo safe to explore on macOS or Windows?

Yes for reading and learning. No for real build/apply operations.

## What should I run first?

Read the README, then Course 01, then the dry-run guide.
